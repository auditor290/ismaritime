About Us

We have several years of experience in serving commercial/leisure oceangoing vessels with the supply, installation and servicing of all types of Marine Navigational, Safety and Communication equipment, such as: Gyro-compass, Radar, Echosounder, GPS, SSAS, GMDSS equipment (EPIRB/AIS/NAVTEX), INMARSAT C, FLEETBROADBAND, Marine V-SAT, MF/HF, DSC-VHF, Handheld Radio, Speed Logs, SVDR/VDR, ECDIS, Magnetic Compass, Dynamic Positioning Systems (DPS) etc.
